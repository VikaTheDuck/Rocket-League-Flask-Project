Welcome to Rocket League Time!

This is a small website about everything you need to know to start playing Rocket League. Described as "soccer, but with rocket-powered cars", Rocket League has up to four players assigned to each of the two teams, using rocket-powered vehicles to hit a ball into their opponent's goal and score points over the course of a match. A match lasts typically five minutes long, but can be longer if the is an overtime. The game was developped and published by Psyonix and was releades for the first time in July 2015 for PS4 and Windows.
